Alberta School Compliance & Quality Assurance Review

Our comprehensive evaluation assesses governance, policies, teaching practices, student support systems, and operational compliance.

We provide a clear action plan to ensure your school is fully prepared for accreditation, inspections, and ongoing accountability.

  • 1
    Compliance Scan (Surface-Level)

    Quick overview of all domains identifying major risks.

  • 2
    Deep-Dive Audit

    Full review across all 8 domains including documentation and interviews.

  • 3
    Action Plan

    Clear roadmap of what’s missing, what’s non-compliant, and what to fix first.

Core Compliance Domains

  • ✓ Governance & Leadership: Board structure, policy governance.
  • ✓ Legislative & Regulatory: Alberta Education Act alignment.
  • ✓ Accreditation & Assurance: Self-study readiness.
  • ✓ Policy & Procedure: Student, staff, and inclusion policies.
  • ✓ Teaching & Learning: Alberta curriculum alignment.
  • ✓ Student Assessment: Report cards aligned with AB standards.
  • ✓ Inclusive Education: IPPs and supports for diverse learners.
  • ✓ Health, Safety & Ops: Emergency procedures and facility compliance.

Why Retention Matters

For independent schools, replacing a student or a great educator costs significantly more than retaining them. High turnover disrupts the community and impacts your bottom line.

Our proactive frameworks help you build a culture where families want to stay and top-tier teachers want to build their careers.
